Detailed Metrics

47 data points analysed

Click any metric to expand the full analysis, recommendation, and expected impact.

Page Speed
42
Critical

Your site takes 4.2 seconds to load on average mobile connections. Google's threshold for "good" is under 2.5s. You are currently in the bottom 25% of sites in your category.

Why it matters: A 1-second improvement in page load time increases mobile conversions by 7% on average (Google, 2023).
Recommendation
Compress and convert images to WebP/AVIF, eliminate render-blocking resources, and implement lazy loading for below-the-fold content. Target: <2.5s LCP.
Accessibility
61
High

Multiple WCAG 2.1 AA failures detected including missing form labels, insufficient colour contrast on 8 elements, and missing keyboard focus indicators on interactive components.

Why it matters: 1 in 6 people globally has a disability. WCAG failures also create legal exposure in the EU, UK, and US.
Recommendation
Audit all form fields for explicit labels, raise contrast ratios to minimum 4.5:1 for body text and 3:1 for large text, add visible focus rings to all interactive elements.
Mobile UX
48
Critical

Your mobile experience has 6 tap target violations, horizontal overflow on 2 pages, and text smaller than 12px in 4 locations. 60% of your traffic is on mobile.

Why it matters: Poor mobile UX is the single largest driver of cart abandonment for e-commerce sites under $10M ARR.
Recommendation
Implement a mobile-first design pass. Start with the checkout flow — fix tap targets, eliminate overflow, and increase font sizes to minimum 14px for body text.
Core Web Vitals
55
High

LCP: 4.2s (Poor), FID: 180ms (Needs improvement), CLS: 0.18 (Poor). All three Core Web Vitals are below Google's "Good" threshold — this directly impacts your search rankings.

Why it matters: Google uses CWV as a ranking signal. Sites with "Good" CWV scores rank 20% higher on average for competitive keywords.
Recommendation
Prioritise LCP: preload hero image, reduce server response time. Fix CLS: reserve space for ads, images, and embeds. Target: LCP <2.5s, CLS <0.1, FID <100ms.
Conversion UX
67
Medium

CTA hierarchy is unclear on the homepage — three competing primary actions are visible above the fold. Trust indicators are absent from the checkout flow, increasing abandonment risk.

Why it matters: Clear CTA hierarchy and checkout trust signals (security badges, reviews) increase checkout completion by 12–18%.
Recommendation
Define a single primary CTA per page. Add trust signals (SSL badge, return policy, customer reviews) adjacent to the checkout button.
SEO Signals
78
Good

Meta titles and descriptions are present on 94% of pages. Structured data is implemented for products. One issue: 3 pages have duplicate H1 tags and 8 images are missing alt text.

Why it matters: Clean SEO signals improve crawlability and ranking — fixing the remaining issues could improve organic traffic by 5–10%.
Recommendation
Fix duplicate H1s on product category pages. Add descriptive alt text to all product images. These are quick wins with meaningful SEO impact.
